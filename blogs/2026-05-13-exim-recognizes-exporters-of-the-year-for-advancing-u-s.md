The Export-Import Bank of the United States (EXIM) has named its Exporters of the Year , honoring 10 U.S. companies whose sustained export performance, global reach, and partnership with EXIM have helped strengthen American competitiveness in markets around the world. The award recognizes exporters across a range of industries who have demonstrated consistent international growth while leveraging EXIM support to manage risk and expand access to global customers.
