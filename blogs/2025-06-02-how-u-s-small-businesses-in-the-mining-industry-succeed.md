The mining industry serves as the foundation for manufacturing, as raw materials derived from mines are the keystone of supply chains in many industries. EXIM has supported many U.S. small businesses providing solutions to the mining industry in expanding their customer base and reaching international markets, including: GeoPhysical Technology (GTI): This Houston, TX-based manufacturer of geophysical technology creates seismic sensor products that illuminate and monitor the earth’s subsurface.
